Interface ITile

  • All Superinterfaces:
    IAppearance, IPosition

    public interface ITile
extends IPosition, IAppearance
    Immutable representation of a tile of the dungeon. This doesn't include monsters present on the tile.
    See Also:
    ILevel.monsters(), ILevel.monsterAt(IPosition)
